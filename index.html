Privacy Policy for Math AI Buddy Last Updated: January 11, 2026 Introduction Math AI Buddy ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application. Information We Collect 1. Photos and Camera Access • We request access to your device's camera to capture photos of homework assignments • We request access to your photo library to select existing images • Photos are processed temporarily to generate completed homework and are NOT stored on our servers • Processed images are saved directly to your device's photo library 2. Purchase Information • We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases (credit packs) • RevenueCat collects transaction data necessary to fulfill your purchase • We do not have access to your payment card details—these are handled securely by Apple 3. Local Device Storage • We store your credit balance and usage status locally on your device using AsyncStorage • This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server How We Use Your Information • Photo Processing: Images you capture or select are sent to our AI service (Nano Banana/Google Gemini) solely to analyze and complete homework problems. Images are processed in real-time and are not retained after processing. • Purchase Fulfillment: Transaction data is used to deliver credits to your account. • App Functionality: Local storage is used to track your available credits and free trial status. Processed in real-time and immediately discarded after generating results. • Purchase Records: Maintained by Apple and RevenueCat per their respective policies. • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app. Data Security We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information: • All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption • Photos are transmitted securely and not stored on external servers • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's secure infrastructure Children's Privacy Math AI Buddy is designed to help parents assist children with homework.
